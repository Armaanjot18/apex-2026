A New File Has Been Opened. GNE'S APEX 2026

THIS ARCHIVE DOCUMENTS AN EXHILARATING AND HIGHLY AWAITED INTER-SCHOOL MULTI-EVENT COMPETITION, PROUDLY ORGANIZED BY THE ESTEEMED CAUSMIC CLUB UNDER THE AEGIS OF THE DEPARTMENT OF APPLIED SCIENCES, GURU NANAK DEV ENGINEERING COLLEGE, LUDHIANA.

INTRODUCTION

GNE’S APEX 2026 aims to extend the college’s educational vision beyond its campus walls, promoting deeper inter-institutional bonds and offering a platform for students from various schools to explore, express, and evolve. Adding color and vibrancy to the day, the APEX FAIR will transform the campus into a festive wonderland filled with cultural performances, decorative lights, and the joyous spirit of this festival season. The fair will feature an engaging assortment of stalls offering traditional crafts, festive delicacies, fun games, and interactive activities to delight every visitor.

At the core of GNE’S APEX 2026 is the heartfelt intention to discover and nurture the hidden talents of young learners. By participating in a wide range of events and competitions, students will have the chance to express their abilities in a welcoming and empowering environment.
